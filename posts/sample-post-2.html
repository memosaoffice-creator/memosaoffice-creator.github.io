How to Speed Up a Slow Android Phone

Published: Nov 27, 2025 • 950 words • GadgetPulse

If your Android phone is lagging, don’t panic. Try these easy steps to bring back speed and smoothness without buying a new device.

1. Restart and update

Restarting clears temporary files. Install system updates for bug fixes and performance improvements.

2. Remove heavy apps

Uninstall apps you don't use and disable bloatware where possible.

3. Free up storage

Keep at least 10-20% free storage. Delete big files or move them to cloud storage.

4. Check for malware

Run a reputable mobile antivirus or scan for unknown apps.
